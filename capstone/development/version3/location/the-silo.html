The Silo

Rephoto of Old Silo, Davis (Ansel Adams Archive, 1967)

The Silo was one of the first buildings built on the university campus in 1909. Its original use was as a dairy barn until 1965 and has since been repurposed into a space for eating and studying.

Recommendations from a Silo regular: Spokes Grill’s Loaded Spokes are a decent alternative to In-N-Out’s Animal Style Fries and the lines are much shorter :)
