The Big Bike

Rephoto of Davis University California Penny-Farthing Bicycle Statue Postcard (1970s)

The Big Bike is a sculpture of a Penny-farthing bicycle constructed by the metal shop class at Davis Senior High School in 1975. It is near the area where one of the first bike lanes in Davis and in the United States were installed "on Sycamore Lane (between Russell Blvd. and W. 8th St.)" (City of Davis website).
