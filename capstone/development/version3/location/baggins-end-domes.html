Baggins End Domes

Rephoto of Mailboxes (Baggins End Archive, 1976)

The Baggins End Domes is a cluster of 14 white domes housing a community of UC Davis students. Built on campus by its first student residents in 1972, the domes were originally identifiable by different exterior colors. It has been noted as a historical landmark and an "excellent example of 1970s eco-architecture and communal lifestyle" (DavisWiki).
