Sprocket Bike Tunnel

The Sprocket Bike Tunnel is located under La Rue Road along the Sprocket Bikeway and connects campus to West Davis. The downward slope into the tunnel offers a fun boost in speed for bicyclists. Due to its lower elevation, the tunnel is prone to being flooded in the heavy rain season.
