Welcome to
Photohistory

Survey of the Davis Bike Loop

Photohistory is a Davis community rephotographic survey project focused on locations of interest along the Davis Bike Loop.

Embark on a scavenger hunt to find each site and answer location specific prompts to unlock the history and photos associated with these Davis landmarks. Hint: Each prompt is designed to be answered in one word.

Have fun!
