Arboretum GATEway Garden

Rephoto of Photo by Katie Hetrick (2015)

The Arboretum GATEway Garden serves as the entrance to the UC Davis Arboretum and connects the campus to downtown Davis. Built in 2014, the garden showcases native plants and bioswales to protect water quality, as well as a bike tunnel mural and art installations. According to the UC Davis Arboretum and Public Garden, the Shovel Gateway sculpture welcomes visitors in and "dovetails with city and campus goals to improve pedestrian and bicycle connections and to promote the campus and downtown area as an arts destination."
